AI & Data Safety

How VociApp uses AI, what data may be sent for processing, and how to report issues.

AI Features in VociApp

VociApp may provide optional AI-assisted features, including:

  • answer feedback during study
  • mnemonics, usage examples, synonyms, or etymology-style learning help
  • term analysis and verb detection
  • verb conjugation support
  • context generation for vocabulary
  • AI-assisted CSV/OCR vocabulary extraction and import enrichment
  • admin-assisted deck enrichment and quality checks

What May Be Sent to AI Processing

Depending on the feature, VociApp may send selected content to a Supabase Edge Function and then to OpenAI, such as:

  • the word, phrase, verb, or card you asked about
  • your answer and the expected answer for feedback
  • deck language pair and context needed to generate a useful response
  • recognized OCR text or imported text you selected for AI-assisted extraction
  • technical request metadata needed for quota enforcement, diagnostics, and cost control

AI Output Is Not Guaranteed

AI-generated content can be wrong, incomplete, biased, or inappropriate. VociApp is a study aid, not an authoritative dictionary, certified translation tool, professional tutor, exam authority, legal adviser, medical adviser, financial adviser, or safety-critical system.

Prohibited Uses

Do not use VociApp AI features to create, request, or distribute:

  • harassment, bullying, hate, sexual, exploitative, or violent content
  • content involving child exploitation or abuse
  • deceptive, fraudulent, impersonation, or deepfake content
  • malicious code, evasion instructions, or abuse-enabling content
  • official documents, credentials, or statements intended to deceive
  • content that violates applicable law or the rights of others

Report AI Problems

If AI output is harmful, offensive, unsafe, clearly wrong, or violates these rules, report it to vociapp.support@gmail.com. Include:

  • the feature used
  • the approximate time and date
  • the prompt or card text, if you are comfortable sharing it
  • the problematic output
  • why it is harmful, wrong, or unsafe

Privacy and AI

Do not submit sensitive personal information, secrets, passwords, health information, financial information, private documents, or information about other people into AI prompts or imports unless you are comfortable with it being processed by the service providers used to answer your request.

For complete data handling details, read the Privacy Policy.
