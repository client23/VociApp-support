Account deletion

Request deletion of your VociApp account

Use this page if you want to delete your VociApp account and associated server-side account data.

Important

Deleting your VociApp account removes account-scoped server-side data where deletion is legally and technically possible. It does not automatically cancel an App Store or Google Play subscription. Manage active subscriptions in your Apple ID or Google Play account settings before or after requesting deletion.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email vociapp.support@gmail.com with the subject line VociApp Account Deletion Request.
  2. Send the request from the email address used for your VociApp account, if possible.
  3. Include the VociApp account email address and state that you want your account deleted.
  4. Do not send your password. We will never ask for your password by email.

What Will Be Deleted

For a verified account deletion request, we will delete or anonymize account-associated server-side data where legally and technically possible, including:

  • VociApp account/profile records
  • synced decks, cards, review logs, learning progress, settings, stats, and related account-scoped learning data
  • AI entitlement records and non-essential AI usage records linked to the account where deletion is permitted
  • deck subscription relationships and account-specific synced content relationships

What May Be Retained

Some records may be retained where necessary for legitimate reasons, such as:

  • security, abuse prevention, fraud prevention, and audit logs
  • billing, subscription, refund, tax, or dispute records held by Apple or Google
  • legal compliance or enforcement of terms
  • backup records until they expire through normal backup retention cycles
  • support emails related to the deletion request or prior support cases, where retention is reasonably necessary

Local Device Data

Account deletion affects server-side account data. Data stored only on your device may remain until you delete it locally. To remove local data, delete the app from your device or use available in-app delete/reset features.

Guest-Local Data

If you used VociApp without an account, there may be no server-side account data to delete. Guest-local data is stored on your device and can be removed by deleting local data or uninstalling the app.

Expected Timing

We aim to acknowledge deletion requests within 30 days where practical and to complete verified deletion requests within a reasonably quick period required by applicable law. Complex requests, identity verification, backup expiration, legal retention, fraud prevention, or platform billing dependencies may take longer.

Verification may be required to protect your account from unauthorized deletion. We will not ask for your password.

Subscriptions Before Deletion

If you have an active App Store or Google Play subscription, cancel it through Apple or Google if you do not want renewal. Deleting your VociApp account does not control Apple ID or Google Play billing records.

Privacy Policy

For broader data handling, retention, and privacy rights information, read the Privacy Policy.
