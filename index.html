Language learning, local-first

Build vocabulary, review smarter, and keep control of your learning data.

VociApp is a vocabulary trainer for personal language learning. It supports local study, spaced repetition, deck and card management, optional account sync, OCR-assisted import, and optional AI-powered learning support.

Local-first vocabulary decks, cards, review history, SRS progress, and study settings.
Optional account registration enables Supabase-backed sync and account-specific AI quota.
Optional AI features help with explanations, context, vocabulary extraction, verb analysis, and import enrichment.
Camera/photo OCR import is user-initiated and used to extract vocabulary from selected learning material.
